Volunteer

Sambhaavnaa welcomes volunteers from various backgrounds to contribute their time and passion to its projects, workshops and programs as well as the farm and other campus activities.

Collaborate

You can collaborate with us in various capacities towards a diverse range of workshops, programs as well as summer and winter schools on social activism, energy conservation, organic farming and other such things.

Campus Life

At Sambhaavnaa, one is exposed to an active and productive campus life, where you can work on our farms, our school - Udaan, and on all our active and upcoming programs.
